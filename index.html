About Me

Hello, I’m Harley Jackson, a Computer Science student at Hope Liverpool University. With two years left until I graduate, I’m honing my skills in front-end development—focusing on building dynamic, engaging websites and interactive tools. My technical toolkit includes HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python, which I actively use to create intuitive, user-friendly web experiences.

I’m passionate about continuously evolving my coding knowledge, tackling complex projects that challenge my problem-solving skills and push me to stay ahead of emerging trends in development. Whether it’s experimenting with new JavaScript frameworks or fine-tuning CSS animations, I thrive on making the web not just functional, but enjoyable to interact with.

Outside the world of code, I enjoy gaming, following Premier League football, and testing my strategic thinking with chess and puzzles. Staying active—whether mentally or physically—keeps me sharp, focused, and ready for the next challenge.
